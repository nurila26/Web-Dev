Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Milk1 cup
Egg1
Baking powder1 tsp
Sugar2 tbsp
Butter2 tbsp, melted

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.

Step 2: Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.

Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.

Step 4: Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.

Step 5: Serve warm with syrup or fruits.
